# Tameable Frost Dragons Mod 🐉❄️

**Current Version:** `0.0.100`

## Primary Goal
The primary goal of this mod is to introduce **fully tameable and rideable Dragons**, allowing players to command and traverse the world atop these legendary apex creatures.

## Current Features ✅
1. **Dragons are fully functional mounts**  
2. **Taming system** is implemented and working  
3. Core **riding mechanics** are stable and usable  
4. Dragons **spawn naturally** in Snow Biomes  
5. They **work… kind of** 🐣

---

## Current Limitations & Known Issues ⚠️
1. **Behavioral Identity**  
   Dragons currently inherit livestock behavior, causing them to **flee from combat** rather than engage. They behave more like prey than apex predators.  

2. **Animation Gaps**  
   Full animation sets exist, but dragon-specific mounting animations are not fully implemented. Temporary **horse animations** are being used.  

3. **Inappropriate Idle Behavior**  
   Dragons may display **grazing behavior**, breaking immersion.  

4. **Combat Functionality**  
   Dragons lack fully implemented attack behaviors. They **default to fleeing** to prevent premature death.  

5. **Lack of Presence**  
   Dragons are currently **timid**, not yet reflecting the dominance expected of “Apex Kings of the Sky.”  

6. **AI Confusion**  
   Grazing and other livestock behaviors persist in AI logic, which doesn’t match intended apex predator behavior.  

7. **Spawning Limitations**  
   Dragons cannot currently spawn naturally. For now, **admin commands** are required to add them to the world.
